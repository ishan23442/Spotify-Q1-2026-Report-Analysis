# Spotify Q1 2026 Business & Financial Performance Analysis

A detailed analysis of Spotify’s Q1 2026 financial results and business strategy based on the official shareholder update.

### 📊 Project Overview
This project breaks down Spotify’s two-sided audio platform model (Freemium + Premium), key performance metrics, revenue streams, and growth strategy. It highlights the company’s successful transition from user growth to profitable scaling.

### Key Sections
- Business Model & Flywheel Analysis
- Revenue Breakdown (Premium vs Advertising)
- User Growth Metrics (761M MAUs, 293M Subscribers)
- Profitability & Margin Expansion
- Cash Flow & Balance Sheet Strength
- Q2 2026 Outlook
- Competitive Advantage vs Apple Music, YouTube Music & TikTok

- KPI & Growth Metrics Interpretation
- Strategic Insights & Competitive Analysis
- Professional Report Writing
